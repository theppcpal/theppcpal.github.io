The Process

From zero to leads
in four steps

Most local businesses have never run Google Ads. Here's exactly what happens when you work with me — no surprises, no black boxes.

1
Free Audit

I research your business, your competitors, and your local search landscape. You get a clear PDF showing exactly what's possible — free, no strings attached.

2
Campaign Setup

I build your campaigns, write the ad copy, install conversion tracking, and set up call recording. Usually live within a week of starting.

3
Go Live

Ads go live. I monitor daily during the first two weeks, optimising bids and keywords based on what the data actually tells us.

4
Monthly Report

A plain-English report every month — calls generated, cost per lead, what I changed and why. No dashboards to log into, no jargon to decode.
