Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
Plain English summary: This site collects minimal data. If you fill in the contact form, your details go to me directly via Formspree so I can respond. If you accept cookies, Google Analytics helps me understand how visitors use the site — no personal data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing.

Who we are

The PPC Pal is a sole trader business operated by Jordan, based in Sherburn-in-Elmet, North Yorkshire. You can contact us at contact@theppcpal.co.uk.

What data we collect

We collect data in the following ways:

  • Contact form: When you submit an enquiry, we collect your name, email address, phone number (optional), business name (optional) and message. This is processed by Formspree and sent directly to us.
  • Analytics (if you consent): We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use this website. This may include pages visited, time on site, browser type and approximate location. This data is anonymised and aggregated — we cannot identify you personally from it.
  • Advertising tracking (if you consent): We use Google Ads conversion tracking to measure whether visitors who clicked our ads go on to contact us. No personal data is shared with Google beyond what is needed to attribute conversions.

Legal basis for processing

  • Contact form data: Processed on the basis of legitimate interest (responding to your enquiry) and, where applicable, to take steps prior to entering a contract.
  • Analytics and advertising cookies: Processed only with your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your cookies and declining on your next visit.

Third-party services

  • Formspree (formspree.io) — processes contact form submissions. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics 4 — website analytics, only active with your consent. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads — conversion tracking, only active with your consent.
  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces for this website. Google may log your IP address as part of this request. Google Privacy Policy

How long we keep your data

  • Contact form enquiries are kept for as long as is necessary to respond to and manage your enquiry, and no longer than 2 years.
  • Analytics data is retained in Google Analytics for 14 months.
  • If you become a client, a separate data processing agreement will apply.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our use of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for analytics/advertising cookies)
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@theppcpal.co.uk.

Cookies

This website uses cookies in the following ways:

  • Essential: A small cookie stores your cookie consent preference so we don't ask you every time.
  • Analytics (consent required): Google Analytics sets cookies to track visits and behaviour on this site.
  • Advertising (consent required): Google Ads sets cookies to track whether visits from ads result in contact form submissions.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and making a fresh choice when you next visit.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. We will not reduce your rights under this policy without explicit notice.
